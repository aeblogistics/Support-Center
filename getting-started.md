# Step-by-Step Guide to Getting Started with AEB Logistics' Services

> Get started with AEB Logistics: Request, Accept Quote, Make Payment,
> and Book Shipment. Account manager support available.

Quick Start Guide to AEB Logistics Services

To begin utilizing the services offered by AEB Logistics,
please follow these steps:

## Request a Quote

Visit the following link for instructions on how to request a quote:
[AEB Dashboard Quote Request](https://support.aeblogistics.com/article/aeb-dashboard-quote-request).

## Accept the Quote

Once your account manager sends you the quote through your AEB portal,
you will receive an email notification. If you are satisfied with the
quote, click the **"Accept Quote"** button located at the top-left
corner of the quote page. AEB Logistics will automatically generate
an invoice for your payment and order confirmation.

## Make the Payment

The invoice will be sent to you automatically through your AEB portal,
and you will receive an email notification. You can contact your account
manager to discuss the preferred payment method:

| Payment Method | Fee |
|---------------|-----|
| PayPal | 5% surcharge |
| Bank Transfer | No fee |

## Booking

Once payment is received, a shipment will be available in your AEB portal.
The portal offers various useful features:

- 📦 Shipment Details
- 🔍 Tracking
- 📁 File Uploading
- 💬 Conversations

AEB Logistics will stay in contact with the supplier and keep you
informed about any progress.
